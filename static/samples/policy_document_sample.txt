TERMS AND CONDITIONS
--------------------

DEFINITION OF DISABILITY
Own Occupation Period: During the first 24 months of disability, you are considered disabled if you are unable to perform the material duties of your own occupation due to injury or sickness.

Any Occupation Period: After 24 months, you are considered disabled if you are unable to perform any gainful occupation for which you are reasonably fitted by education, training, or experience.

ELIMINATION PERIOD
The elimination period is 90 days of continuous disability. Benefits begin on the 91st day.

BENEFIT AMOUNT
Your monthly benefit is 60% of your monthly pre-disability earnings, up to a maximum monthly benefit of $10,000.

Minimum Monthly Benefit: $100 or 10% of gross monthly benefit, whichever is greater.

MAXIMUM BENEFIT PERIOD
Benefits are payable for the duration of disability up to:
- To age 65, or
- Based on age at disability per the Maximum Benefit Period Schedule

EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS
--------------------------
This policy does not cover disabilities caused by or resulting from:

1. Pre-existing Conditions: Any condition for which you received treatment, consultation, or diagnostic services within 3 months prior to your coverage effective date. This exclusion applies for the first 12 months of coverage.

2. Intentional self-inflicted injury or attempted suicide.

3. War or act of war (declared or undeclared).

4. Commission of or attempt to commit a felony.

5. Any period of incarceration.

6. Normal pregnancy or childbirth (complications of pregnancy are covered).

7. Cosmetic surgery unless medically necessary.

8. Injuries sustained while operating a motor vehicle under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

REHABILITATION AND RETURN TO WORK
---------------------------------
We may require you to participate in a rehabilitation program if we determine it would be beneficial to your recovery. We will pay for the cost of approved rehabilitation programs.

Work Incentive Benefit: If you return to work in a limited capacity, you may receive a partial benefit equal to your full monthly benefit minus 50% of your return-to-work earnings, for up to 24 months.

CLAIMS PROCEDURES
-----------------
1. Report disability within 30 days of onset.
2. Submit completed claim form with medical documentation.
3. Claimant and attending physician must provide ongoing documentation as requested.
4. Benefits are paid monthly in arrears.

TERMINATION OF COVERAGE
-----------------------
Coverage terminates on the earliest of:
- Termination of employment
- Termination of the Group Policy
- Cessation of premium payments
- End of the class eligibility period
