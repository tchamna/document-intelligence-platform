SCOPE OF WORK
-------------

1. OVERVIEW
Metro Health Systems is seeking proposals from qualified vendors to provide comprehensive group benefits administration services for our organization of approximately 5,000 employees across 12 locations in the Midwest region.

2. SERVICES REQUIRED

2.1 Enrollment Administration
- Annual open enrollment processing
- New hire enrollment within 30 days
- Qualifying life event changes
- COBRA administration
- Online enrollment portal with employee self-service

2.2 Claims Administration
- Medical, dental, and vision claims processing
- Disability claims administration (STD and LTD)
- Life insurance claims processing
- Target turnaround: 5 business days for routine claims
- Appeals processing and management

2.3 Customer Service
- Dedicated service team for Metro Health
- Toll-free number for employee inquiries
- Online portal with chat support
- Service level: 95% of calls answered within 30 seconds
- Hours: Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM CST

2.4 Reporting and Analytics
- Monthly utilization reports
- Quarterly claims analysis
- Annual benchmarking reports
- Ad-hoc reporting capabilities
- Executive dashboard

2.5 Compliance
- ERISA compliance monitoring
- ACA reporting and filing
- HIPAA compliance
- State insurance regulations
- DOL audit support

3. REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Technical Requirements
- Cloud-based platform with 99.9% uptime SLA
- Integration with our HRIS system (Workday)
- API access for data exchange
- Mobile app for employees
- Single sign-on (SSO) capability

3.2 Experience Requirements
- Minimum 10 years in benefits administration
- Experience with healthcare organizations preferred
- Current clients of similar size (3,000-10,000 employees)
- References from at least 3 current clients

3.3 Security Requirements
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- Annual penetration testing
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Business continuity/disaster recovery plan

4. PRICING

Please provide pricing for the following:

4.1 Implementation Fees
- One-time setup and configuration
- Data migration
- Training

4.2 Ongoing Administration Fees
- Per Employee Per Month (PEPM) pricing
- Tiered pricing if applicable
- Optional services pricing

4.3 Additional Services
- Custom reporting
- Additional integrations
- After-hours support

5. PROPOSAL FORMAT

Proposals must include:
- Executive Summary (2 pages max)
- Company Background and Qualifications
- Technical Solution Description
- Implementation Plan and Timeline
- Staffing and Account Management Structure
- Pricing Proposal (separate sealed document)
- Sample Reports
- References (minimum 3)
- Sample Service Level Agreement

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated on:
- Experience and Qualifications (25%)
- Technical Capabilities (25%)
- Service Approach (20%)
- Pricing (20%)
- References (10%)
